# Azure AI-900 Practice Quiz

This comprehensive practice quiz is designed to help you prepare for the Microsoft Azure AI-900 exam. It includes 50 multiple-choice questions covering all major topics from the skills measured outline (updated May 2, 2025).
Questions are a mix of conceptual, scenario-based, and Azure-specific to mimic the exam format. Aim to complete it in 60 minutes. Select the best answer for each.

Answers are provided at the end with brief explanations. Use this to identify weak areas and review the study guide.
